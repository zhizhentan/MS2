illus:

Product & UX design
Aug.2017 - Sep.2017
Personal work

Intro:
illus is a handheld monochrome printer providing local print for people who work with both paperbased traditional media and digital equipment.

Highlight:
Rather than providing a constant printing speed, the innovative printer measures the relative distance between the resting hand of its user and itself to determine accordingly what to print as the distance increases.

This new approach creates a smaller, portable printer and gives rise to a more convenient working experience.
